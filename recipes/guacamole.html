Creamy Guacamole

This easy guacamole recipe is quick and simple to make. Great with tortilla chips or as a topping for fajitas!

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados, peeled and pitted/li>
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients in a bowl and enjoy with chips!
