El Toro Cheese Dip

Smooth Queso.. with a twist!

El Toro cheese dip was a favorite in Florence, Alabama. It's great with a big basket of tortilla chips or french fries.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp dried minced onion
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder/li>
  • 1/2 tsp red/cayenne pepper
  • 3/4 tsp cumin
  • 1 lb original Velveeta cheese
  • 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Mix together ingredients in a microwave safe bowl
  2. Microwave on high for 5 minutes, stopping every minute to stir
  3. Serve and enjoy!
